# QR Code Pro - Scan & Generate

# Description

QR Code Pro – Scan & Generate is a fast and reliable QR code scanner, reader, and generator designed for every need. Scan any QR code instantly and access smart actions like opening URLs, connecting to Wi-Fi, saving contacts, viewing social profiles, sending messages, and more.

You can also create custom QR codes for links, Wi-Fi, contacts, text, social media, and anything else you want to share.

ALL FORMATS

Scan all popular QR codes: QR, Data Matrix, PDF417, Aztec, and much more.
The app automatically recognizes the content and shows the most relevant action.

SMART & FAST QR SCANNER

Perform instant actions for every scanned code:
- Open URLs safely
- Connect to Wi-Fi networks
- Save vCard or MeCard contacts
- Add calendar events
- View product info
- Access social media profiles
- Send messages or emails
- Show locations or map coordinates

SCAN FROM IMAGES

Scan QR codes from gallery images or detect them directly using the camera.

FLASH SUPPORT

Use the flashlight in dark environments.

POWERFUL QR CODE GENERATOR

Create and share custom QR codes for:
- Websites (URLs)
- Wi-Fi networks
- Contacts (vCard / MeCard)
- Phone numbers & SMS
- Email
- Social media profiles
- Locations
- Text and more

Display your QR on screen or share it instantly.

FULL SCAN HISTORY

Keep track of all your past scans with an organized history. Reopen actions, check details, and manage your saved QR codes easily.

SUPPORTED QR CODE TYPES

- Website links
- Contact data (vCard, MeCard, VCF)
- Calendar events
- Wi-Fi access
- Geolocation
- Phone & SMS
- Email, MATMSG
- PIX payment codes
- Social platforms (WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, X/Twitter, YouTube, LinkedIn, Discord, and more)

CUSTOMIZABLE SETTINGS

Tailor the app to your preferences with flexible options:
- Enable or disable flash controls
- Allow or block QR scanning from gallery images
- Show or hide raw data from scan history
- Configure behavior for automatic actions

These tools let you choose a cleaner interface, more privacy, or faster workflows—your app, your way.

PRIVACY & PERFORMANCE

Built with secure scanning technology and optimized for fast detection, low latency, and safe link handling.
Works offline for most QR code types and requires minimal permissions.
